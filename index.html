👋 Who I Am

I'm a Data Scientist at Tesco in London, where I own large-scale personalisation systems on Azure ML — from reward allocation serving 6M+ customers across the UK and Ireland, to contextual bandit models that delivered a 20%+ CTR uplift in live A/B tests.

Alongside industry work, I'm a Research Fellow at Apart Research, working on LLM safety — building mechanistically interpretable safety judges and applying quality-diversity search to adversarial red teaming across high-stakes domains.

My foundation is an MSc in Computational Statistics and Machine Learning from UCL (Distinction, 82.3%) and a BSc in Physics from CUHK (First Class, 3.7 GPA). I gravitate toward probabilistic modelling, production ML engineering, and anything at the intersection of maths and real-world impact.
